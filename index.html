Industries

A single platform, configured for three operational worlds.

Ground Support Equipment (GSE)

Tow tractors, GPUs, loaders, tugs, de-icers. Track utilization, gate response, battery/engine health, and automate inspections & preventive maintenance.

Trucking & Fleet

Trucks, trailers, forklifts. Driver/vehicle events, mileage, service cycles, compliance logs, and predictive downtime reduction.

Aviation MRO

Tools, ground assets, components. Work packages, inspection traceability, parts accountability, and audit-ready documentation.
